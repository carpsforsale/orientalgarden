About Us

Welcome to ORIENTAL GARDEN located at 1160 N 38th St, Broken Arrow, OK 74014.

We take pride in serving the most authentic and delicious Chinese cuisine in Broken Arrow, OK.

Our menu features a range of Chinese dishes, including General Tso's chicken, Sweet & Sour Chicken, and beef with broccoli. Each dish is prepared with a careful balance of seasonings and ingredients to create a unique and satisfying flavor profile. We use only the freshest ingredients, resulting in a truly sublime dining experience.

In addition to great food, we also prioritize providing an enjoyable dining experience. We warmly invite you to savor our authentic Chinese dishes firsthand!
